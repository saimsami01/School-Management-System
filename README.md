## ✨ Key Features

- 🔐 **Secure Login** — Role-based access (Admin, Principal, Teacher, Accountant, Receptionist), password hashing, and "Remember Me"
- 📊 **Live Dashboard** — Student/staff counts, fee collection, attendance summary, and admissions trend chart
- 🎓 **Student Management** — Complete student profiles with photo, birth certificate, medical notes, transport/hostel status, auto-computed age, instant search, and filters
- 📥 **Bulk Excel Import** — Import hundreds of students within minutes
- 👩‍🏫 **Teacher & Staff Management** — Qualifications, salary, attendance, and contact records
- 🏷️ **Classes, Sections & Subjects** — Complete academic structure management
- 📅 **Attendance** — One-click daily attendance for students, teachers, and staff
- 💰 **Fee Management** — Configurable fee structures, bulk bill generation, receipts, and pending fee tracking
- 📝 **Examinations** — Quiz, assignment, and exam weighted grading with automatic GPA and class position calculation
- 🗓️ **Timetable**
- 📚 **Library Management**
- 📦 **Inventory Management**
- 🚌 **Transport Management**
- 📄 **Reports** — PDF & Excel export
- 💾 **Automatic Backups** — Scheduled local and cloud backup support
- 🔒 **Offline License Activation** — Hardware-locked licensing
- 🌓 **Dark & Light Mode**
